Workshops and advising

Did you know it’s a radical act to devote three hours to yourself every week?

Join me in March (on Zoom) on Tuesdays for SIX sessions from 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM PST (8:00 PM - 11:00 PM EST).

Dates: March 3, 10, 17, 24, 31*, April 7

*Please note: There will be a beautiful substitute, Callan, on March 31st.

All you need is a phone or a computer, a comfortable setting, and yourself. Come and go as you please. Cameras can be on or off. I created Night Moves as a digital group space to “get shit done.” Instead, it became a space that fosters a gentle, safe, pressure-free sense of artsy togetherness while individuals explore what it is they really need. Some people come to simply practice what it feels like to carve out time for themselves. Other people come to deepen intuition and self-trust around how to spend the time. Either way, it’s nothing short of magic. All participants will receive one email from me a week containing our schedule and Zoom link. About Joanna “Sky” Fusco

I’m Joanna Fusco

an artist working across drawing, writing, and a public practice I call Internet Caretaking. Some people call me Sky. Other people just call me Lordcowboy.

I have a commercial studio called ROCK SHOP located in Morro Bay, California.

Visitors are welcome on Fridays and Saturdays from 11-3 at 735 Napa Ave. Check Instagram for updated hours or write me an email at Hello@lordcowboy.com to make an in-person appointment.

What is ‘Unsupervised’?

Wild at heart, weird on top, and publicly learning to love myself. With over 25,000 subscribers, Unsupervised is my Substack newsletter. These letters make frequent stops at personal histories, relationships, communal living, addiction, making art, & navigating the digital Anthropocene. It ain't much but it's honest work.
